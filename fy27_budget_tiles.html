What is a city budget?

Think of it as Atlanta's annual plan

A city budget is simply a financial plan — a document that says: here's the money we expect to bring in, and here's how we'll spend it to run the city and invest in its future.

Like a household budget

Income comes in, bills get paid, and what's left gets invested in the future — just at city scale.

A one-year plan

The budget covers July 2026 through June 2027 — one fiscal year of decisions and commitments.

A set of priorities

Every dollar spent is a choice. This budget reflects what Atlanta has decided matters most right now.
